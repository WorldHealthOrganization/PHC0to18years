Prevention of falls

• Provide adequate adult supervision in unsafe environments

Infants and young children

• Modify or replace unsafe objects (strollers, baby walkers, high chairs, changing tables, baby exercisers). This includes replacing changing tables with changing mats, reducing the height and use of guard rails on bunk beds.

• Modifying unsafe environments in the home. Install safety equipment such as window bars, stair gates, balcony guards that cannot be climbed and safety glass in windows.

DO NOT use hazard-prone products such as baby walkers.

Older children

• Ensure your child wears a helmet and wrist- and mouthguards during activities such as biking, inline skating, skateboarding.
