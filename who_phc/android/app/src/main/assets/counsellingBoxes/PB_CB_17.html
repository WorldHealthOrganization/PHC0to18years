Kangaroo mother care is beneficial for your
baby’s development

• Dress your baby only in a nappy, hat and socks. Aim for a core body temperature of 36–37 °C, with the feet warm and pink.

• Place your baby in skin-to-skin contact on your chest between the breasts, with your child’s head turned to one side.

• Tie the baby to your body with a cloth and cover yourself and your baby with your clothes.

• Breastfeed your child frequently.

• While fathers obviously cannot breastfeed their newborns, they can provide excellent kangaroo care.
