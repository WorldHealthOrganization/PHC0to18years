How to care for your child with pneumonia at home

• Give paracetamol or ibuprofen if your child has high fever (≥ 39 °C) that causes distress.

• Make sure your child drinks enough and give extra fluids or breast milk if there is fever. Small frequent drinks are more likely to be tolerated and less likely to be vomited.

• Keep your child in a semi-seated position if your child has trouble breathing.

DO NOT smoke around your child.

• Return after 2–3 days for re-evaluation or earlier if your child is breathing fast, has difficulty in breathing, becomes sicker or is unable to drink or breastfeed.
