How to care for your child with varicella at home

• Apply calamine lotion to relieve itchiness.

• Dress your child in long-sleeved and -legged clothes, and keep nails cut short to avoid scratching (reduces risk of infections and scarring of the skin).

• Tell your child not to scratch the lesions.

• Give paracetamol if the child has high fever (≥ 39 °C) that causes distress.

• Make sure your child drinks more than usual.

• Return if the fever persists after 2–3 days, or earlier if your child worsens or is unable to drink or breastfeed.
