How to avoid tick bites and what to do if they occur

• Ensure your child wears long sleeves and long trousers when playing in grassy areas or in the forest. After playing outside, undress your child and examine the clothes and entire body including skin folds for ticks and tick bites.

• In the event of a tick bite, remove the tick as soon as possible (see image).

• Seek medical advice when symptoms (such as erythema migrans) develop.

Removing a tick: remove the tick with a clean fine-tipped tweezer, by grasping the tick as close to the skin as possible. Pull upward with steady, even pressure. Do not twist the tick.
